How the Pick Up App works

We created an app called Pickup. Pickup functions as an app that allows users to either create a pickup game or RSVP for one. So let’s say you want to create a recurring game every Wednesday at 6:00 pm. You would be able to do just that. Say you want to charge an entrance fee to be able to reserve the fields, you can do that. The app will also be designed to protect the safety of players from unwanted guests knowing the location.
